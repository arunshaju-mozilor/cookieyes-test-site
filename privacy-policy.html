2. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently and to provide information to site owners.

3. Types of cookies we use

  • Strictly Necessary: Required for the site to function. Cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics: Help us understand how visitors use the site (e.g. Google Analytics).
  • Marketing: Used to track visitors and serve relevant ads.
  • Functional: Enable personalisation and enhanced features.

4. Managing your preferences

You can update your cookie preferences at any time by clicking the "Manage Cookies" link in the cookie banner or footer.
